Prof. Dr. Gopal Bahadur KC
Senior Research Specialist (Agriculture)
Expertise: Plant Pathology, Integrated Pest Management, Mycotoxin Analysis
Biography
Prof. Dr. Gopal Bahadur KC is a leading figure in Nepal's agricultural research and education landscape, with over 33 years of experience in plant pathology and sustainable farming practices. Born on December 27, 1957, in Lamjung, Nepal, Dr. KC is a Gold Medalist graduate from the Institute of Agriculture and Animal Science (IAAS), Tribhuvan University, and holds an MScAg and PhD in Plant Pathology from Georg-August University, Goettingen, Germany.
